About Me

I was born on July 8th, 1988 in Pequannock, New Jersey. Pequannock is a small town in Northern New Jersey with a poplution of 13,888. I attended a Catholic Elementary School from Pre-K through 4th grade. For 5th grade, I had decided to make the switch to the townships public middle school, Pequannock Valley, for grades 5-8. Upon graduation middle school, I had decided to continue with public education at Pequannock Highschool, for grades 9-12. Switching from a small catholic school into the larger public education system provided me with better social skills more friendships than if I had stayed in the catholic school.

Up next was COLLEGE!!!! I decided to attend Marist College in Poughkeepsie, NY. After being accepted into the Business School, I determined that I wanted a concentration in both Finance and Marketing. My first job out of school was at an Investment Bank. After three years at the bank, I realized that banking was not for me so I decided to see what the Insurance world had to offer. After two years of being an insurance broker, I took notice that the previous two jobs were more or less being a paper pushing between clients and vendors. Not being thrilled with this, I began to have an interest in programming. Which is why I am currently enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp at the Jersey City location.
